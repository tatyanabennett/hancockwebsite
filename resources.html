    • Act and Course Info
      Scoring higher on the ACT can drastically improve the odds of getting accepted into the university of your choice and winning more scholarship awards. The resources below will better prepare you for the ACT and provide you with a list of high school classes to help prepare you for college.
    • Apply for Free Money
      Free money for higher education is everywhere! People, clubs, schools & organizations are eager to award this FREE money to those who qualify. HHS encourages you to apply for these opportunities along with awesome federal programs like FAFSA (Federal Financial Aid).
    • Research Colleges
      Not all college curricula are created equally. Research and discover which universities are best suited to help you reach your future career goals. Investing a small amount of time now can potentially save you lots of money and frustration during and after your college years.
